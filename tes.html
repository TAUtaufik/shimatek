1. Press the Power ON/OFF button to turn on the fan. 2. Press the (+) button to increase the fan speed and wait until the number on the screen stops flashing. 3. Press the (-) button to decrease the fan speed and wait until the number on the screen stops flashing. 4. To turn off the fan, make sure the fan speed is set to 1 (one) on the screen. 5. Do not turn off the fan when it is at full speed. Instructions:
